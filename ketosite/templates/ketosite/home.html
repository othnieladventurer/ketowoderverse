Keto Diet 101: Things you need to know.

What is the Keto Diet?

The Keto Diet is a low-carb, high-fat eating plan designed to induce ketosis. In this metabolic state, the body burns fat for energy. Learn about its principles and science.

The Benefits of the Keto diet

The Keto Diet, suitable for beginners, promotes weight loss, sustained energy, mental clarity, appetite control, and metabolic health benefits. With potential therapeutic applications, it emphasizes nutrient-dense foods and fats. It's crucial for beginners to consult healthcare professionals for personalized guidance.

Is the Keto Diet Safe?

When done properly, the Keto Diet is generally safe. However, caution is advised for those with specific health concerns. Consultation with a healthcare professional is crucial for a safe keto journey.
